About the Exam:

This exam is carried out to evaluate your proficieny in the language across five key language dimensions.

  • Time Limit: 60 Minutes
  • Question Types: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Result: Immediate feedback with star ratings

Testing your skills

Exam Levels & Skills

Comprehensive evaluation of the following skills is carried out:

  1. Reading: Analyzing text and comprehension
  2. Listening: Understanding the language
  3. Grammar: Orthographic and Linguistic Rules
  4. Speaking & composition: Language Structure
